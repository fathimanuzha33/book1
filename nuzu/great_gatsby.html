The Great Gatsby

A novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald that explores themes of decadence and excess in 1920s America.

Published in 1925, it is set in the Jazz Age on Long Island and in New York City, and it tells the story of Jay Gatsby's quest to win back his former lover, Daisy Buchanan.

The Great Gatsby is often regarded as a critical reflection of the American Dream.
